## 4. Category & Content Rating

### 4.1 Primary Category Selection

| Platform | Primary Category | Secondary Category | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Apple App Store** | Games > Role Playing | Games > Adventure | 7/12 competitors use Roleplaying; RPG category has higher browse traffic than Adventure on iOS. Secondary category (Adventure) captures the remaining search intent. |
| **Google Play** | Game > Role Playing | -- | Google Play allows only one category. Role Playing chosen over Adventure because RPG category browsers have higher engagement with text-heavy games (Eldrum, AI Game Master both in RPG). |

**Why not "Adventure" as primary?**
- The Adventure category on both stores is dominated by graphical point-and-click games (e.g., Monument Valley, The Room).
- Text-heavy AI games cluster in Roleplaying (AI Game Master, StoryZone, FableAI, AdventureAI, Maze AI, Eldrum -- 6 of our 12 competitors).
- Roleplaying category browsers expect and accept text-centric interfaces.
