DANTE TERMINAL is a mobile text adventure where an on-device AI serves as your Game Master. No cloud. No internet. No subscriptions. Just you, a retro terminal, and an AI that understands whatever you type.


Think Zork meets modern AI — running entirely in your pocket.

  • 100% offline — AI runs on your device after a one-time model download
  • Type anything — no fixed verb lists, the AI understands natural language
  • Retro terminal UI with typewriter text, scanlines, and that green glow
  • Handcrafted adventures with puzzles, NPCs, and multiple endings
  • No ads. No tracking. No cloud dependency. Your game, your device.
